Survival Multiplayer Server

When TechnoBuffalo launched the Minecraft Server back in 2011, survival multiplayer was the only gamemode that we supported. We had one map, two at times, and kept it near and dear to our hearts. Almost 3 years later, we are ready to bring you back to the enjoyable days of Minecraft at TechnoBuffalo.

The survival server is the most heavily played server on our cluster of 4 servers and like the others, run continuously 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.

We pack many different plugins on the survival server in order to keep the game dynamic, competitive, and enjoyable enough to retain your interest. A complete (approved) list of plugins that we use can be found below, in addition to a list of commands that will allow you to use them.

Rank Information

Little Buffalo

Little Buffalo is the begginning rank for new players. Little Buffalos are stricken to the minimal amount of amenities. The player must prove that they are capable of following the rules, and understand the game to be eligible to rank up.

The requirements to rank up to Buffalo are:

Playtime: 12 hours/.5 days
Money: $250

Buffalo

Buffalo is the second rank in the player rank ladder. Players are given this title after they have proved themselves by meeting the requirements of the Little Buffalo rank. By this point, the player should know the basics about the server, including all rules and regulations.

The requirements to rank up to Big Buffalo are:

Playtime: 60 hours/2.5 days
Money: $1,000.00

Big Buffalo

Big Buffalo is the third rank in the player rank ladder. Players are considered to be experienced by the time they reach this level in the rank ladder. They now will be allowed to use the Residence system by creating residences for their own use.

The requirements to rank up to Explorer are:

Playtime: 120 hours/5 days
Money: $5,000.00

Explorer

Explorer is the rank that follows Big Buffalo, and is the first rank of the second tier. Players that reach this level will receive a green rank title, as well as a green username. Reaching this level shows that you have explored the map, and put in enough playtime to have successfully earned enough TechnoBuffalo Bux.

The requirements to rank up to Merchant are:

Playtime: 180 hours/7.5 days
Money: $10,000

Merchant

Merchant the fifth rank on the ladder of ranks, and requires a proven track record of saving up money to reach this level along with the completion of 20 days of playtime. By this time, you should have some continual or consistent form of income, to give you a boost to the required dollar value.

The requirements to rank up to Baron are:

Playtime: 240 hours/10 days
Money: $15,000

Baron

Baron is the final rank of the second tier in the player rank ladder. Now that you are at the top of the middle bracket of ranks, you have significant knowledge of the server, the rules, and its players. We hope that at this point, you pay it forward. All of the knowledge you know about how to play on the server, can be handed down to a new player by taking a few minutes out to assist them. You are recognized for your assistance, and knowledge of the intricate workings of the server.

The requirements to rank up to Knight are:

Playtime: 180 hours/20 days
Money: $25,000

Veteran

Veteran is an unobtainable rank in the player rank ladder. Only players who have played on the server's 1.7 Beta map starting in July of 2011, will have the opportunity to be automatically promoted to Veteran. While Veteran is no longer an attainable rank otherwise, it falls between the Baron and Knight ranks.

The requirements to rank up to Knight are:

Playtime: 600 hours/25 days
Money: $25,000

Knight

Knight is the first rank of the third tier in the player rank ladder. If available, server staff will host a Knighting ceremony, to welcome you into the third and final tier of ranks. This is the beginning of the final journey to reaching the final rank of Elder. Significant dedication and time must be devoted to play in order to reach this tier and rank. And for that, you are a loyal player to TechnoBuffalo. You are now an honorable Knight.

The requirements to rank up to Duke are:

Playtime: 720 hours/30 days
Money: $50,000

Duke

Duke is the second highest rank on the player rank ladder. Reaching this rank shows skill, coupled with passion. You are a regular, and know mostly everybody by name, and likewise. You are recognized for your devotion to this server.
The requirements to rank up to Elder are:

Playtime: 1440 hours/60 days
Money: $100,000

Elder

Elder is the final rank in the player rank ladder. As the elder rank, you have demonstrated significant dedication and strategy to have achieved the server's most prestigious rank. Elders will be able to access special commands including fly, and many other surprises! Congratulations on making it all the way up the ladder! We thank you for your continued support and dedication! This rank is typically for ex-staff members.
