Benefits of Playing Here

Play For Free

We know that you work hard to build anything that your mind can think of, and we know that you come here to play hassle-free. So why be bothered with advertisements and donation buttons while trying to play? We won't do that. Not now, not ever. We're sponosored by TechnoBuffalo, and won't ever ask for your hard earned [real] money.

Always Up-to-Date

We have a blog page which will be updated with any relevant information pertaining to the server. Whenever we have scheduled maintenance, or plan to add or modify something, we will always keep you in the know.

Player Protection

We understand that with any public server, there will be those people who receive enjoyment from disrupting it. We like to combat that, plain and simple. We utilize anti-griefing systems, as well as a sophisticated ban management system that has dramatically decreased the amount of rogue players. We've got you covered.
