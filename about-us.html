About Us

This server is the official Minecraft server of TechnoBuffalo. It was first turned on in July of 2011 when Minecraft was in its Beta 1.7 version, when the game was in its infancy. Since, the server has grown through numerous upgrades, changes and additions to make the experience that much more entertaining. Jon Rettinger, TechnoBuffalo's founder believed that Minecraft is a place where fans of TechnoBuffalo and Minecraft can enjoy the game and technology together.

Coming up on its 3 year anniversary, the TechnoBuffalo Minecraft crew decided that it was time for a re-launch. The launching of this website marks the next generation of the TechnoBuffalo Minecraft experience.

          Why Play Here?

          Tiered Achievements

          There are many ways that you can unlock new features within the server by accomplishing tasks. For each task that you accomplish, you become one step closer to ranking higher within the TechnoBuffalo hierarchy.

          Plan And Create

          Minecraft is the number one game for expanding your creative ability and imagination. Thinking outside of the box and actually being able to create that idea gives you the power to experiment and think in new ways. Create anything that you wish alongside some of the best builders in Minecraft.

          Aesthetics

          Making your build look like it belongs in the real world is the last piece to the creative puzzle that you can use to stretch the ides of reality. Making your creations look the way you want with no outstanding budget or restrictions is something that you can only experience here on Minecraft at TechnoBuffalo.
