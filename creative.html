Creative Server

TechnoBuffalo introduced creative mode in 2012. The world of Minecraft was changing, and to stay ahead of the curve, the creative server was born! Initially it was a free build creative map just like survival is. We quickly realized, that won't do. So again, after careful analysis, we decided to make it better.

The implementation of PlotMe allowed players to have plots (clever name) all throughout the map in pre-determined locations. This was the first step to making the creative server more organized and self-sufficient. From that point forward, there was no need for moderation, and players could come and go freely with a sense of security, knowing that their builds would always be safe.

Players flock to the creative server to build freely, as well as test designs for the survival server. You may come and do as you wish, and it is always available for your convenience.

Rank Information

Hub

Hub is the starting rank for new players on the creative server. Hub ranked players are stricken to the minimal amount of amenities. The player must prove that they are capable of following the rules to rank up.

Artisan

Artisan is the second rank in the creative server rank ladder. Players are given this title after they have proved themselves by meeting the requirements of the Hub rank. By this point, the player should know the basics about the creative server, including all rules and requirements.

Builder

Builder is the third rank in the creative server rank ladder. Players are considered to be experienced by the time they reach this level in the rank ladder.

The requirements to rank up to Mason are:

Your build will be judged by staff.

Mason

Mason is the top rank in the creative server rank ladder. Playes have demonstrated their creative ability and can now utilize World Edit within their plots. Congratulations on becoming such a creative Minecrafter!
